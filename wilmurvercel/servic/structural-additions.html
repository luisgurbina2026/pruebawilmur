Home Addition FAQs

How long does a home addition project take? expand_more

A standard single-story room addition takes about 6 to 10 weeks. Second-story pop-top additions or large multi-room wings require detailed structural engineering and can take 12 to 16 weeks from excavation to finish.

Do you handle architectural planning and engineering? expand_more

Yes. We coordinate with structural engineers and architects to draft blueprints that comply with local zoning codes, load-bearing weight guidelines, and wind velocity standards.

Can I stay in my home during construction? expand_more

For single-story extensions, yes. We partition the build site to minimize dust and noise. For second-story additions, we recommend temporary lodging since the main roof is removed during framing.

Will my utility systems need to be upgraded? expand_more

Depending on the square footage added, we check if your furnace/AC, electrical panel, and main water lines have the capacity or require upgrades to feed the new structural zones.
