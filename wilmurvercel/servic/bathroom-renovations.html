Bathroom Renovation FAQs

How long does a bathroom renovation project take? expand_more

A standard bathroom renovation typically takes between 3 to 5 weeks. Master bathroom suites requiring layout redesigns, extensive tile patterns, custom glass, or high-end plumbing relocations can take 5 to 7 weeks.

Can you design a curbless walk-in shower? expand_more

Yes. We specialize in curbless, barrier-free walk-in showers featuring linear drains, glass panel enclosures, and custom waterproofing systems (such as Schluter-Kerdishield) for modern, leak-free design.

Do you offer heated floor options? expand_more

Yes, we install electric radiant floor heating systems underneath tile and stone floors. These systems are energy efficient and controlled by a digital, programmable thermostat.

What waterproofing systems do you use? expand_more

We strictly use industry-leading waterproofing membranes and systems, primarily Schluter Systems (Kerdi membrane and boards) to ensure your shower is 100% water-tight and mold-resistant for life.
