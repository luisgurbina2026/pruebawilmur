Decks & Patios FAQs

What deck materials do you recommend? expand_more

We highly recommend capped composite decking (like Trex or AZEK) for low-maintenance longevity and fade resistance. For natural wood enthusiasts, we build with sustainable tropical hardwoods like Ipe or premium cedar.

How long does it take to build a custom deck? expand_more

A standard ground-level deck takes 1 to 2 weeks. Multi-level decks, custom pergolas, or projects with integrated stone patios and outdoor kitchens take between 3 to 5 weeks to complete.

Do I need a permit to replace an existing deck? expand_more

Yes, building codes in Chicago and surrounding suburbs require permits for structural deck building or replacement to ensure safety compliance (ledger attachment, footings, and rail height).

Do composite decks require maintenance? expand_more

Composite decks require virtually no staining, painting, or sealing. Simply wash with mild soap and water twice a year to clear surface dirt and pollen.
