Since 1984, we've been crafting homes that tell a story. Not just of wood and stone, but of the families who live within them.

Our Story

What started as a small workshop in a backyard garage has grown into a cornerstone of the local community. Wilmur Construction is a family-owned business passed down through two generations of master craftsmen.

We don't just see ourselves as contractors; we are partners in your life's biggest investment. Our decades of experience have taught us that while styles change, the need for honest craftsmanship and open communication never does.
