Handyman FAQs

What tasks does a handyman handle? expand_more

Our handyman crew handles drywall patching, custom crown molding, door hanging, light fixture installations, sink/faucet swaps, TV wall mounting, smart home configurations, and general interior/exterior repairs.

Do you charge by the hour or by the job? expand_more

For small, quick repairs we provide straightforward hourly structures. For larger lists of tasks, built-in installations, or paint projects, we draft solid line-item estimates so you know the full cost in advance.

Are your handymen background checked? expand_more

Yes, every team member is fully background-checked, uniformed, and trained under our strict precision standards. We carry comprehensive general liability insurance.

Is there a minimum trip charge? expand_more

Yes, to account for technician travel and equipment staging, we have a standard 2-hour minimum service charge for small call-out lists.
